FUEL PRESSURE NORMALIZATION 
1. Connect the fuel pump unit 5P (Black) 
connector [1]. 
Connect the battery negative (–) cable . 
2. Turn the ignition switch ON. 
The fuel pump will run for about 2 seconds 
and fuel pressure will rise. 

NOTE: 
* Do not start the engine. 
3. Turn the ignition switch OFF. 
4. Repeat previous step 2 or 3 times, and check 
that there is no leakage in the fuel supply 
system. 
5. Install the fuel tank .
